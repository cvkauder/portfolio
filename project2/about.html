About

Catherine Valenti Kauder is a freelance writer, web and graphic designer based in the SF East Bay area. She has worked as a journalist for many news outlets, including ABCNEWS.com, TheStreet.com and Dow Jones Newswires, where many of her stories were published in The Wall Street Journal. Her freelance articles have appeared in publications such as Glamour, Ladies Home Journal and All You magazines. She is currently studying web and graphic design at Diablo Valley College and has designed web sites and logos for small businesses and non-profits in the bay area.
